RV mini-split questions

Will a mini-split work in my RV? +

Yes — we install in Class A, Class C, travel trailers, 5th wheels, and cargo/toy hauler conversions. We'll assess your rig and recommend the right size and mounting approach.

Can I run it on a 30-amp hookup? +

Absolutely. Our 9k and 12k BTU units run on a single 20-amp circuit, leaving plenty of headroom on a 30-amp hookup for everything else.

Where does the outdoor unit go? +

Depending on your rig, we mount it on the tongue, bumper, frame rail, or a custom bracket. We'll recommend the best spot during your consultation — it won't affect towing.

Does it work on solar / boondocking? +

Mini-splits draw significantly less power than rooftop units. With a decent lithium battery bank (200Ah+) and solar, many owners run them off-grid for hours. We'll help you size it.

Can I remove the rooftop AC after? +

Yes — many owners remove the rooftop unit to save weight (80-100 lbs), reduce drag, and improve gas mileage. We can advise on capping the roof opening.

What's the warranty? +

5-year parts, 2-year labor. Compressor covered up to 10 years. Same warranty as our residential installs.
